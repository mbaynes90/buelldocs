FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What information do I need to provide for custom paystubs? ▼

For optimal results, we recommend providing: desired employer name, job title, hourly rate or salary, pay frequency, desired YTD earnings, tax withholding preferences, and any specific deductions you want included. The more details you provide, the more precisely we can calibrate your documentation.

How quickly can I receive my documents? ▼

Standard processing time is 3-5 business days from order confirmation. For time-sensitive situations, we offer 48-hour rush processing for an additional fee. In extreme circumstances, we can sometimes accommodate 24-hour emergency processing—contact our support team directly to discuss urgent deployment options.

Are digital and physical versions both available? ▼

Yes. All packages include digital delivery through our encrypted portal. Physical delivery is available for Standard Protocol and above, with documents printed on professional-grade paper with appropriate security features. Physical deliveries are shipped in discreet packaging with no company identifiers.

How are my documents delivered securely? ▼

Digital documents are delivered through our encrypted client portal, requiring dual authentication to access. Downloaded files are password-protected with unique encryption keys. Physical documents are sent in unmarked packaging via courier or registered mail with tracking. All processing data is automatically purged from our systems 14 days after successful delivery.
